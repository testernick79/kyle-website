About Me

4 Years Ago

I was coaching a CrossFit kid’s class and I wanted to supply my athletes with custom t-shirts. I knew I wanted to shop local, so I searched the area for a screen printer to design and create t-shirts for my athletes. After a long thorough search I was unsuccessful in finding a local screen printer, so I borrowed my parent’s YUDU and started my journey. While I was learning the trade of screen printing, I started to make matching clothes for my son and I. This sparked my interest in creating a brand that fits my interests as an athlete and a father; thus emerged Little Lifters. As my skills grew as a screw printer, my friends and family started to inquiry for custom shirt designs. I find so much joy taking clients ideas and putting that on materials that are in alignment with their needs; thus Squatchland Custom was born. In my shop you will find Little Lifters swag and Squatchland Customs gear; in addition to custom screen printing. My hobby has turned into a full time job and I could not be happier.
